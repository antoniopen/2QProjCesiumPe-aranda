Drive to Survive

 

 

The sport of Formula 1 gives birth to rivalries both on and off the track. From team politics and management issues to driver competition and pressure, watch as all of the chaos unfolds in this Netflix exclusive series. This documentary gives its viewers the rare opportunity of looking into the true and crazy stories of those fighting to be on top of the world’s greatest racing competition.

The Ultimate Guide to Formula 1 for New Fans

 

When first getting into this sport, things can get confusing, and very much so. Because of that, here’s a guide that will get you started in no time! Made by CYMotorsport on YouTube, this video is specifically designed for newer fans, or even those who are completely out of the loop, who haven’t found a proper and comprehensive explanation of what F1 truly is. It tackles Formula One’s history and roots, the teams, their technology, the format, and all the other details that are important for a newcomer.

For more high quality content such as this, check out their YouTube channel!
